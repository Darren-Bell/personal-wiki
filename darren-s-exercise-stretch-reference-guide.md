## WARM-UP (Do Before Every Session)

### 1. Cat-Cow Stretch
**Why:** Mobilises the entire spine, relieves back stiffness
**Sets/Reps:** x 10 reps (slow, controlled)

**Key cues:**
- On all fours, hands under shoulders, knees under hips
- INHALE: Drop belly, lift head and tailbone (cow)
- EXHALE: Round spine, tuck chin and tailbone (cat)
- Move SLOWLY — feel each vertebra

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cat+cow+stretch+proper+form
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Squat University

---

### 2. World's Greatest Stretch
**Why:** Opens hips, thoracic spine, and hamstrings in one movement
**Sets/Reps:** x 5 each side

**Key cues:**
- Lunge forward, place opposite hand on the floor inside your foot
- Rotate torso and reach opposite arm to the ceiling
- Hold 2-3 seconds at the top
- Keep back knee off the ground for extra stretch

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=world%27s+greatest+stretch+tutorial
Recommended channels: Tom Merrick, Squat University, ATHLEAN-X

---

### 3. Standing Hip Circles
**Why:** Loosens hip joints, reduces morning stiffness
**Sets/Reps:** x 10 each direction, each leg

**Key cues:**
- Stand on one leg (hold wall for balance)
- Lift opposite knee to hip height
- Draw large circles with your knee
- Keep circles controlled, not fast

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=standing+hip+circles+exercise
Recommended channels: FitnessFAQs, Calisthenicmovement

---

### 4. Glute Bridges
**Why:** Activates glutes (they're asleep from sitting all day!)
**Sets/Reps:** x 15 reps

**Key cues:**
- Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat on floor
- Push through HEELS, squeeze glutes at the top
- Hold top position for 2 seconds
- Don't hyperextend your lower back

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=glute+bridge+proper+form
Recommended channels: Mind Pump TV, ATHLEAN-X, Jeff Nippard

---

### 5. Dead Bugs
**Why:** Core stability WITHOUT straining your back
**Sets/Reps:** x 10 each side

**Key cues:**
- Lie on back, arms straight up, knees at 90 degrees
- Press lower back INTO the floor (this is critical!)
- Slowly extend opposite arm and leg
- Return to start, repeat other side
- If your back arches off the floor, you've gone too far

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=dead+bug+exercise+tutorial
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Squat University

## STRETCHES (Your Main Focus Right Now)

These are your core stretches. Do them every day after the warm-up.

### 6. Pigeon Stretch
**Why:** Deep hip flexor and glute stretch — essential for your back
**Hold:** 60 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- From all fours, bring one knee forward behind your wrist
- Extend the other leg straight behind you
- Keep hips square (facing forward)
- Lean forward over the front leg for deeper stretch
- If this is too intense, do it lying on your back (figure-4 stretch)

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=pigeon+stretch+tutorial
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Tom Merrick

---

### 7. Seated Hamstring Stretch
**Why:** Loosens tight hamstrings (prevents pulls during jogging)
**Hold:** 60 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- Sit on the floor, one leg extended, other foot against inner thigh
- Reach towards your extended foot
- Hinge at the hips, don't round your back
- You should feel it in the middle of the hamstring, not behind the knee

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=seated+hamstring+stretch+proper+form
Recommended channels: Tom Merrick, Yoga With Adriene

---

### 8. Hip Flexor Lunge Stretch
**Why:** Opens up tight hip flexors from sitting at a desk
**Hold:** 60 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- Kneel on one knee (pad it if needed)
- Other foot forward, knee at 90 degrees
- Push hips FORWARD gently
- Squeeze the glute of the kneeling leg
- You should feel the stretch in the front of the hip
- Keep torso upright — don't lean forward

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=hip+flexor+lunge+stretch
Recommended channels: Squat University, Tom Merrick

---

### 9. Child's Pose
**Why:** Gentle spinal decompression, back relief
**Hold:** 60 seconds

**Key cues:**
- Kneel on the floor, sit back on your heels
- Reach arms forward on the floor
- Let your forehead rest on the ground
- Breathe deeply into your lower back
- Walk your hands to each side for a lateral stretch

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=child%27s+pose+stretch
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene

---

### 10. Doorway Chest Opener
**Why:** Counteracts rounded shoulders from desk work
**Hold:** 30 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- Stand in a doorway, forearm against the frame
- Step through the doorway gently
- You should feel the stretch across your chest and front shoulder
- Adjust arm height (low, mid, high) to hit different fibres

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=doorway+chest+opener+stretch
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Tom Merrick

## YOGA MOBILITY FLOW (Add After Stretches)

These yoga poses complement your stretches. Do them after the stretches above, or as a standalone 15-20 min session on rest days (without the warm-up).

### 11. Downward Dog → Cobra Flow
**Why:** Spinal decompression + back extension — directly targets morning stiffness
**Sets/Reps:** 5-6 flows (no hold, continuous movement)

**Key cues:**
- Push up to Downward Dog from all fours (knees can be bent)
- Pedal your feet to loosen hamstrings
- Slowly lower to the floor, leading with your chest
- Push up into a gentle Cobra (hips stay on the floor)
- Breathe: exhale into Down Dog, inhale into Cobra
- Move slowly — feel each vertebra

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=downward+dog+to+cobra+flow+beginners
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Tom Merrick

---

### 12. Supine Spinal Twist (Jathara Parivartanasana)
**Why:** Releases lower back tension, improves rotational mobility for golf
**Hold:** 60 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- Lie on back, hug one knee to chest
- Let the knee fall across the body to the floor
- Extend opposite arm out to the side, look away from the knee
- Let gravity do the work — don't force the knee down
- Keep both shoulders on the floor
- Breathe deeply into the stretch

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=supine+spinal+twist+yoga+tutorial
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Brett Larkin Yoga

---

### 13. Thread the Needle
**Why:** Thoracic rotation — directly transfers to your golf swing
**Hold:** 30-45 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- Start on all fours
- Reach one arm under your body, rotating your torso
- Rest the shoulder of the reaching arm on the floor
- Let your head follow the reaching arm
- You should feel it through your upper back and shoulder
- Move slowly into and out of position

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=thread+the+needle+stretch+yoga
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Tom Merrick

---

### 14. Lizard Pose (Utthan Pristhasana)
**Why:** Deeper hip opener than Pigeon — targets the tight hip flexors that cause back pain
**Hold:** 45-60 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- From a lunge, drop both hands inside your front foot
- Stay on hands, or lower to forearms for a deeper stretch
- Front knee tracks directly over ankle — don't let it cave in
- Back knee can stay on the floor (pad it if needed)
- This will feel intense — stay at a comfortable edge, not pain
- Breathe and relax into it

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=lizard+pose+yoga+tutorial
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Brett Larkin Yoga

---

### 15. Reclined Butterfly (Supta Baddha Konasana)
**Why:** Inner thigh and groin release — directly addresses your groin pull history
**Hold:** 90 seconds

**Key cues:**
- Lie on back, bring soles of feet together
- Let knees fall open to the sides naturally
- Arms relaxed by your sides, palms up
- Let gravity pull your knees down — don't force them
- If it's too intense, put a pillow under each knee for support
- Breathe deeply — this is a relaxation pose as much as a stretch

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=reclined+butterfly+pose+supta+baddha+konasana
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Brett Larkin Yoga

---

### 16. Supported Fish Pose
**Why:** Chest and thoracic opener — counteracts desk-job rounding
**Hold:** 60 seconds

**Key cues:**
- Roll a towel into a firm log shape
- Place it across your upper back (around bra line area)
- Lie back over it, arms out wide, palms up
- Adjust towel position until you feel a comfortable stretch across your chest
- Breathe deeply — let your chest expand over the towel
- This is the antidote to sitting at a desk all day

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=supported+fish+pose+yoga+tutorial
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Brett Larkin Yoga

---

### 17. Happy Baby (Ananda Balasana)
**Why:** Hip decompression and lower back release
**Hold:** 60 seconds

**Key cues:**
- Lie on your back
- Grab the outside of your feet (or use a towel/belt around each foot)
- Bring knees wide towards your armpits
- Keep your lower back flat on the floor
- Gently rock side to side — like a kid playing
- Breathe and relax into the hip opening

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=happy+baby+pose+yoga+tutorial
Recommended channels: Yoga With Adriene, Brett Larkin Yoga

## WHEN YOU'RE READY — KETTLEBELL SESSIONS

**When to start:** After 1-2 weeks of consistent stretching, when your back pain and stiffness have improved and your body feels ready. There's no rush — the stretching work is building the foundation.

**How often:** 3-4 sessions per week, replacing one yoga flow day with a kettlebell session.

**Session structure:** Warm-Up (5 mins) → Kettlebell Session (20-25 mins) → Stretches + Yoga Flow (25-30 mins)

---

### SESSION A — Mobility & Activation (e.g. Mon/Thu)

### 18. Kettlebell Goblet Squat
**Why:** Perfect squat pattern with built-in counterbalance
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10 (12kg)

**Key cues:**
- Hold kettlebell at chest level, elbows pointing down
- Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out
- Sit BACK and DOWN, as if into a chair
- Drive knees OUT over toes (don't let them cave in)
- Go as deep as comfortable without pain

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=kettlebell+goblet+squat+proper+form
Recommended channels: Onnit Academy, StrongFirst, Mark Wildman

---

### 19. Kettlebell Romanian Deadlift (RDL)
**Why:** Strengthens posterior chain — hamstrings, glutes, lower back
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10 (12kg)

**Key cues:**
- Stand with feet hip-width apart, kettlebell in front of thighs
- Hinge at the HIPS (push your bum backwards)
- Slight bend in knees, keep them fixed
- Lower until you feel a stretch in your hamstrings
- Squeeze glutes to return to standing
- Your back stays FLAT throughout — never round it

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=kettlebell+romanian+deadlift+tutorial
Recommended channels: Onnit Academy, StrongFirst, Mark Wildman

---

### 20. Kettlebell Halo
**Why:** Shoulder mobility and stability
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 8 each direction

**Key cues:**
- Hold kettlebell by the horns (upside down) at chest height
- Circle the kettlebell around your head
- Keep elbows close to your body
- Move slowly and controlled
- Reverse direction after 8 reps

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=kettlebell+halo+exercise
Recommended channels: Cavemantraining, StrongFirst, Mark Wildman

---

### 21. Side-Lying Clamshells
**Why:** Activates glute medius — critical for hip stability and knee health
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 15 each side

**Key cues:**
- Lie on your side, knees bent at 90 degrees
- Keep feet together, lift top knee apart
- Don't let your hips roll backwards
- Slow and controlled — feel the burn in your side glute
- Add a resistance band later for progression

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=side+lying+clamshells+proper+form
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Squat University

---

### 22. Bird Dog
**Why:** Core stability, balance, and back health
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10 each side

**Key cues:**
- On all fours, brace your core
- Extend opposite arm and leg simultaneously
- Keep your hips level — don't rotate
- Hold for 2-3 seconds at full extension
- Think "long" not "high" — reach, don't lift

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=bird+dog+exercise+proper+form
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Physiotec, Squat University

---

### 23. Pallof Press
**Why:** Anti-rotation core strength — protects your spine
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10 each side

**Key cues:**
- Attach a resistance band to a fixed point (or use a towel anchored in a door)
- Stand sideways to the anchor point
- Hold band at chest, press straight out
- Resist the rotation — that's the exercise
- Slow and controlled return to chest

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=pallof+press+tutorial
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Exos, Jeff Nippard

---

### 24. Standing Calf Raises
**Why:** Strengthens calves to prevent those pulls during jogging
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 15

**Key cues:**
- Stand on the edge of a step (hold rail for balance)
- Rise up on toes as high as possible
- HOLD at the top for 2 seconds
- Lower slowly below the step level for full stretch
- Keep knees straight but not locked

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=standing+calf+raises+proper+form
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Jeff Nippard

---

### SESSION B — Core & Stability (e.g. Tue/Sat)

### 25. Kettlebell Deadlift
**Why:** Full-body strength, safe introduction to deadlift pattern
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 12 (12kg)

**Key cues:**
- Kettlebell between your feet
- Hinge at hips, grip the kettlebell handle
- Drive through heels, stand up tall
- Squeeze glutes at the top
- Lower with control back to the floor

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=kettlebell+deadlift+beginners
Recommended channels: StrongFirst, Onnit Academy, Mark Wildman

---

### 26. Kettlebell Single-Arm Row
**Why:** Back strength, posture correction (counteracts desk-job rounding)
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10 each side

**Key cues:**
- Place one hand and knee on a bench or chair
- Kettlebell in the free hand, arm hanging straight
- Pull the kettlebell to your hip, elbow close to body
- Squeeze your shoulder blade at the top
- Lower slowly — control the negative

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=kettlebell+single+arm+row+tutorial
Recommended channels: StrongFirst, Mark Wildman, ATHLEAN-X

---

### 27. Plank
**Why:** Core endurance — the foundation of everything
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 30 seconds (build to 60)

**Key cues:**
- Forearms on the ground, body in a straight line
- Squeeze glutes, brace core (imagine bracing for a punch)
- Don't let your hips sag or pike up
- BREATHE — don't hold your breath
- If 30 seconds is too hard, start with 15 and build

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=plank+proper+form+tutorial
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Jeff Nippard

---

### 28. Side Plank
**Why:** Oblique and hip stability — protects your back during rotation (golf!)
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 20 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- Forearm on the ground, body in a straight line
- Stack your feet (or stagger them for easier balance)
- Lift hips off the ground, hold
- Keep your body in a straight line — no leaning forward/back

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=side+plank+proper+form
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Jeff Nippard

---

### 29. Kettlebell Swing
**Why:** THE king of kettlebell exercises — posterior chain power, cardio, fat burning
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10

**Key cues:**
- Hinge at hips (NOT squat), grip kettlebell with both hands
- Hike the bell between your legs
- SNAP your hips forward to swing the bell to chest height
- Arms are just ropes — all the power comes from your HIPS
- Let the bell swing back between your legs naturally
- Keep your back FLAT throughout

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=kettlebell+swing+proper+form
Recommended channels: StrongFirst, Mark Wildman, Onnit Academy

---

### 30. Wall Sit
**Why:** Quad endurance, mental toughness
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 30 seconds

**Key cues:**
- Back flat against the wall
- Slide down until thighs are parallel to floor (or as low as comfortable)
- Knees at 90 degrees, directly above ankles
- Keep your weight through your heels
- BREATHE through it

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=wall+sit+exercise+proper+form
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Jeff Nippard

## LATER PHASES — BARBELL & SPORT-SPECIFIC

**When to start:** Weeks 5-8 (barbell), Weeks 9-12 (sport-specific). Only move here once you've built a solid foundation with the stretching and kettlebell work.

---

### PHASE 2: BARBELL EXERCISES (Weeks 5-8)

### 31. Barbell Back Squat
**Why:** The king of lower body strength
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 8 (START WITH JUST THE BAR!)

**Key cues:**
- Bar rests on upper traps (high bar) or rear delts (low bar)
- Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out
- Brace your core, take a deep breath
- Sit DOWN and BACK, knees tracking over toes
- Aim for thighs parallel to floor (or just above)
- Drive through heels to stand up
- YOUR BACK PAIN HISTORY MEANS FORM IS NON-NEGOTIABLE

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=barbell+back+squat+proper+form+beginners
Recommended channels: Jeff Nippard, Mark Rippetoe/Starting Strength, Squat University

---

### 32. Barbell Romanian Deadlift
**Why:** Progression from the kettlebell RDL — heavier posterior chain loading
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 8 (bar + light plates)

**Key cues:**
- Same hip hinge as the kettlebell version
- Grip the bar just outside your knees
- Keep the bar CLOSE to your legs throughout
- Lower until you feel the hamstring stretch
- Squeeze glutes to return to standing
- NEVER round your lower back

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=barbell+romanian+deadlift+tutorial
Recommended channels: Jeff Nippard, ATHLEAN-X, Squat University

---

### 33. Bulgarian Split Squat
**Why:** Single-leg strength, balance, hip mobility — sport-specific for football
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 8 each leg

**Key cues:**
- Rear foot on a bench or chair behind you
- Front foot 2-3 feet ahead of the bench
- Lower until front thigh is parallel to floor
- Keep torso upright
- Drive through the front heel to stand
- Hold dumbbells at your sides for load

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=bulgarian+split+squat+tutorial
Recommended channels: Jeff Nippard, Jeremy Ethier, ATHLEAN-X

---

### 34. Barbell Hip Thrust
**Why:** The BEST glute builder — directly supports your back and golf swing
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 12

**Key cues:**
- Upper back on a bench, barbell across your hips (use a pad!)
- Feet flat on the floor, knees at 90 degrees at the top
- Drive through heels, squeeze glutes HARD at the top
- Full hip extension — your body should be a straight line from shoulders to knees
- Lower with control

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=barbell+hip+thrust+tutorial
Recommended channels: Bret Contreras (the inventor), Jeff Nippard

---

### 35. Copenhagen Plank
**Why:** Inner thigh (adductor) strength — prevents groin strains
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 15 seconds each side

**Key cues:**
- Side plank position, but top foot on a bench
- Bottom leg hangs free or rests against the bench
- Lift hips up, hold
- This will be HARD at first — build up slowly

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=copenhagen+plank+tutorial
Recommended channels: Squat University, ATHLEAN-X

---

### 36. Barbell Overhead Press
**Why:** Upper body strength, shoulder health, posture
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 8 (start light!)

**Key cues:**
- Bar at shoulder height, grip just outside shoulders
- Brace core, squeeze glutes
- Press bar straight UP, move your head out of the way
- Lock out overhead with bar directly over mid-foot
- Lower with control back to shoulders

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=barbell+overhead+press+proper+form
Recommended channels: Mark Rippetoe/Starting Strength, Jeff Nippard, ATHLEAN-X

---

### 37. Dumbbell Bent-Over Row
**Why:** Back thickness, posture, counteracts desk-job rounding
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10 each side

**Key cues:**
- One hand and knee on a bench
- Dumbbell in free hand, arm straight
- Pull dumbbell to your hip, elbow close to body
- Squeeze shoulder blade at the top
- Lower slowly — control the weight down

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=dumbbell+bent+over+row+tutorial
Recommended channels: Jeff Nippard, ATHLEAN-X, Jeremy Ethier

---

### 38. Kettlebell Floor Press
**Why:** Chest and tricep strength, shoulder-safe pressing
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10 each side

**Key cues:**
- Lie on the floor, kettlebell in one hand
- Upper arm rests on the floor at the bottom
- Press the kettlebell straight up
- Lower until upper arm touches the floor
- The floor limits your range of motion — this is SAFER for shoulders

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=kettlebell+floor+press+tutorial
Recommended channels: StrongFirst, Mark Wildman

---

### 39. Barbell Landmine Rotation
**Why:** Rotational power — directly transfers to your golf swing
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 8 each side

**Key cues:**
- Wedge one end of the barbell in a corner (or use a landmine attachment)
- Hold the other end with both hands at chest height
- Rotate your torso, moving the bar in an arc
- Keep hips relatively stable — the rotation comes from your core
- Control the movement both ways

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=barbell+landmine+rotation+tutorial
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Squat University

---

### 40. Turkish Get-Up
**Why:** Full-body stability, shoulder health, mobility — the ultimate functional exercise
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 3 each side (light kettlebell)

**Key cues:**
- Lie on your back, kettlebell in one hand, arm straight up
- Follow these steps SLOWLY:
  1. Roll to your side, prop up on elbow
  2. Push up to hand
  3. Lift hips (bridge)
  4. Sweep leg through to kneeling
  5. Stand up
  6. Reverse the sequence to lie back down
- NEVER take your eyes off the kettlebell
- If this feels too complex, practice WITHOUT weight first

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=turkish+get+up+kettlebell+tutorial
Recommended channels: StrongFirst, Mark Wildman, Onnit Academy

---

### PHASE 3: SPORT-SPECIFIC EXERCISES (Weeks 9-12)

### 41. Lateral Lunges
**Why:** Side-to-side movement for football and golf
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 10 each side

**Key cues:**
- Stand with feet together
- Step wide to one side, bending that knee
- Keep the other leg straight
- Push back to centre
- Keep your chest up throughout

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=lateral+lunges+tutorial
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Jeff Nippard

---

### 42. Kettlebell Clean & Press
**Why:** Full-body power, shoulder strength
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 8 each side

**Key cues:**
- Start with kettlebell on the floor
- Clean it to your shoulder (like a fast deadlift that ends at the shoulder)
- From the shoulder, press it overhead
- Lower to shoulder, then back to the floor
- Keep the kettlebell CLOSE to your body during the clean

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=kettlebell+clean+and+press+tutorial
Recommended channels: StrongFirst, Mark Wildman

---

### 43. Box Jumps / Step-Ups
**Why:** Explosive power, leg strength for football
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 8

**Key cues:**
- Start with a LOW box or step (knee height)
- Jump with both feet, land softly with bent knees
- Stand up fully on the box
- Step down (don't jump down — saves your joints)
- If jumping feels risky, do step-ups instead with dumbbells

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=box+jumps+proper+form
Recommended channels: ATHLEAN-X, Jeff Nippard

---

### 44. Farmer's Walk
**Why:** Grip strength, core stability, full-body conditioning
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 30 metres

**Key cues:**
- Hold heavy dumbbells at your sides
- Stand tall, shoulders back, core braced
- Walk with short, controlled steps
- Don't let the weights pull you forward or to one side
- Breathe normally — don't hold your breath

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=farmers+walk+exercise+tutorial
Recommended channels: Mark Wildman, ATHLEAN-X

---

### 45. Bear Crawl
**Why:** Full-body coordination, core strength, shoulder stability
**Sets/Reps:** 3 x 20 metres

**Key cues:**
- On all fours, knees 1 inch off the ground
- Move opposite hand and foot together
- Keep your back flat — no rocking side to side
- Move SLOWLY at first — this is harder than it looks
- Keep knees close to the ground (don't let them fly up)

Watch: https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=bear+crawl+exercise+tutorial
Recommended channels: FitnessFAQs, ATHLEAN-X

## QUICK REFERENCE: FORM RULES THAT APPLY TO EVERYTHING

- **BACK FLAT** — If your back rounds during ANY exercise, lower the weight or stop
- **BREATHE** — Exhale on the effort (lifting), inhale on the return
- **CONTROL THE NEGATIVE** — The lowering phase is just as important as the lifting
- **PAIN vs DISCOMFORT** — Muscle burn = good. Sharp/joint/nerve pain = STOP
- **WARM UP FIRST** — Never skip it. Your calves and hamstrings depend on it
- **START LIGHT** — Ego lifting is how ex-athletes get hurt. Build up gradually
- **BENT KNEES ARE YOUR FRIEND** — Especially in Down Dog and Forward Fold
- **BREATHE INTO THE STRETCH** — If you're holding your breath, you're pushing too hard
